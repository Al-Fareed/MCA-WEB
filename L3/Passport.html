What's New
PSKs and POPSKs have started resuming the Operations owing to the relaxations given by the States in line with the COVID-19 guidelines. Intended Applicants can book the appointments to the nearest Operational PSK/POPSK. Refer Latest News Section for Operational PSK and POPSK List. In case of emergency/medical cases and if none of the PSKs are operational, applicant may approach the concerned Passport Office. Applicants are advised to wear a mask, carry sanitizer, download and install Aarogya Setu App and follow social distancing norms at PSKs/POPSKs during the visit. ** 176 Indian Mission/Post are connected with Passport Seva System.
